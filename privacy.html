MeeMeeCat — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

MeeMeeCat is a virtual cat pet game made for children. We take privacy seriously, especially for kids.

The short version: MeeMeeCat does not collect, store, or share any personal information. Everything you do in the app stays on your own device.

What we collect

Nothing. MeeMeeCat has:

  • No accounts or sign-ups
  • No analytics or tracking
  • No advertising
  • No third-party software development kits (SDKs)
  • No servers — your cats, gems, badges, and settings are saved only on your device

Sharing features

  • Invite a friend / Share a cat photo / Share a cat code: These use Apple's built-in Share Sheet and only happen when you choose to share. They are placed behind a parental gate (a grown-up must solve a math problem first).
  • A shared "cat code" contains only the cat's appearance (colors, ears, etc.) — never a name or any personal information.
  • We do not transmit any data to us or to any server.

Photos

If you choose "Save Photo," the app creates a picture of your cat and uses the system Share Sheet so you can save it to your own Photos. The app asks permission before adding to your photo library and does not read your existing photos.

Children's privacy

MeeMeeCat is designed to comply with children's privacy rules (such as COPPA in the US and GDPR-K in Europe). Because we collect no data, there is nothing to share with anyone.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be directed to support@tinymaker.studio.
