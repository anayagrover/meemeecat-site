Privacy Policy

MeeMeeCat & Tiny Maker Studio

Last updated: May 31, 2026

The short version

We don't collect, store, or share any personal information. Everything you do in our apps stays on your own device. We take privacy seriously, especially for kids.

What we collect

Nothing. Our apps have:

  • ❌ No accounts or sign-ups
  • ❌ No analytics or tracking
  • ❌ No advertising
  • ❌ No third-party software development kits (SDKs)
  • ❌ No servers — your cats, gems, badges, and settings are saved only on your device

Sharing features

Sharing a cat photo, a "cat code," or an invite uses your device's own Share Sheet and only happens when you choose to share. Sharing features are placed behind a parental gate (a grown-up must solve a math problem first).

A shared "cat code" contains only a cat's appearance (colors, ears, etc.) — never a name or any personal information. We do not transmit any data to us or to any server.

Photos

If you choose "Save Photo," the app creates a picture of your cat and uses the system Share Sheet so you can save it to your own Photos. The app asks permission before adding to your photo library and does not read your existing photos.

Children's privacy

Our apps are designed to comply with children's privacy rules including COPPA in the US, GDPR-K in Europe, and the UK's Age Appropriate Design Code. Because we collect no data, there is nothing to share with anyone.

Changes to this policy

If we ever update this policy, we'll update the date above. The current version is always the one shown here.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email support@tinymaker.studio.
