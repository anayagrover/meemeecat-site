MeeMeeCat

Make & care for cute cats — a cozy, ad-free pet game for kids.

About the app

Design your very own cat, take care of it every day, play fun mini-games, and collect shiny badges. No ads, no accounts, no tracking — everything stays on your device.

Common questions

  • Does it cost anything / have ads? No. MeeMeeCat is free with no ads and no in-app purchases.
  • Is my child's data collected? No data is collected at all. See our Privacy Policy.
  • What devices does it run on? iPhone and iPad running iOS 17 or later.
  • How does sharing work? Sharing a cat photo or "cat code" uses Apple's share sheet and is behind a parental gate. No live data passes between devices.
